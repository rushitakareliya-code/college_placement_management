About Placement Portal

The Placement Portal System is a digital platform designed to simplify the recruitment process between students and companies. It enables students to explore verified job opportunities, apply for positions, and track their career progress through a single centralized platform.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between talented students and leading organizations by providing a transparent, efficient, and reliable placement system.

Through this portal, companies can post job openings, review candidate profiles, and conduct recruitment in a streamlined manner. The system ensures that students receive authentic opportunities and guidance for their professional journey.
