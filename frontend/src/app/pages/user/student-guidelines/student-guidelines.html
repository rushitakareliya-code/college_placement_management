Student Guidelines

  • Always provide accurate personal and academic information.
  • Apply only to positions that match your qualifications.
  • Maintain professional communication with recruiters.
  • Prepare your resume before applying.
  • Check job postings regularly for new opportunities.
  • Attend interviews on time and be prepared.
  • Follow ethical behavior during recruitment.
  • Do not share your account credentials.
  • Ensure your contact information is correct.
  • Respect company recruitment processes.
